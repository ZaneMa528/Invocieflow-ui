Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our e-invoicing platform.

E-invoicing is the exchange of invoice documents between a supplier and a buyer in an integrated electronic format. Unlike traditional paper invoices or PDFs, e-invoices can be processed automatically by the recipient's system, reducing manual handling and errors.

InvoiceFlow provides a user-friendly interface that allows you to create, validate, and send e-invoices. You can upload existing invoice data from CSV/Excel files, manually create invoices through our form, validate them against Australian standards, and send them securely to recipients.

Yes, InvoiceFlow is fully compliant with Australian e-invoicing standards, including the UBL 2.1 XML format required for PEPPOL network transmission. Our validation system ensures that all invoices meet these standards before sending.
