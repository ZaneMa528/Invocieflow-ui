How E-Invoicing Works

Our platform simplifies the e-invoicing process with three easy steps, saving you time and ensuring compliance.

Create

Create e-invoices by uploading CSV/Excel files, filling out a form, or extracting data from PDFs. Our system converts your data into standardized UBL XML format.

1

Validate

Ensure your invoices comply with Australian e-invoicing standards. Our validation system checks for wellformedness, syntax rules, and PEPPOL compliance.

2

Send

Transmit your e-invoices securely via email or SFTP. Track delivery status and maintain a complete history of all sent invoices for easy reference.
